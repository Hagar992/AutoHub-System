Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you provide to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our car rental and sales website.

1. Information We Collect:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Driver's license information
  • Residential address
  • Payment details (e.g., credit card)
  • Geolocation data (when necessary)

2. How We Use Your Information:

  • Process car rental or purchase requests
  • Contact you regarding your booking or purchase
  • Enhance your user experience on the website
  • Send offers or updates (if you opt-in)
  • Ensure safety and comply with legal obligations

3. Data Protection:

We follow strict security procedures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access or misuse. All information is stored securely and encrypted where applicable.

4. Information Sharing:

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • Payment processors for completing transactions
  • Legal authorities when officially requested
  • Insurance companies in case of accidents

5. Cookies:

We use cookies to improve site performance and personalize your experience. You can disable cookies via your browser settings.

6. Your Rights:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Modify or correct your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data at any time

7. Policy Updates:

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here, and your continued use of the site will be considered acceptance of those changes.

For any questions, please contact us at AutoHub@gmail.com.
